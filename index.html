Mathematics For Life Foundation

Mathematics for Life Foundation is a nonprofit organization born out of love for humanity coupled with flair for Mathematics and the act of learning in order to raise and enhance the quality of human life. We encourage and reward students, individuals and institutions that display high level of interests, performance or skills in Mathematics.

About Us

Vision Statement

To be a highly ethical Non-profit organization dedicated to raising the standard of education and eradicating illiteracy in Nigeria.

Mission Statement

To educate every willing Nigerian, using Mathematics as an innovative and unique tool in improving the quality of education across all levels and demonstrate that knowledge of Mathematics can enhance the quality of human life.

Aims and Objectives

1.Increase understanding of Mathematics across all levels of education in Nigeria from play group to higher institution.
2.Encourage and reward students, individuals and institutions... that display high level of interests, performances or skills in Mathematics.
3.Educate school dropouts, illiterates and every willing Nigerian on the importance/uses of Mathematics in everyday life.
4.Train Mathematics teachers/educators in Nigeria so as to give the best to their students.
5.Make learning Mathematics fun and interesting to all students to discourage the myth that Mathematics is difficult to learn or has no real life application.
6.Show students and professionals how Mathematics is related to any field or career they choose to study or are already into and help them understand how to apply it in their various fields.
7.Increase competency in different aspects of Mathematics among student across all levels.
8.Partner with governments and other private sectors in training Nigerians using Mathematics and related fields to help attain a higher level of understanding in their profession.
